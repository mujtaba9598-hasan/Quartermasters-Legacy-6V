# US Agency Pricing Intelligence (Feb 2026)

## Website Redesign (Custom React/Next.js)
- California agencies: $100-$250/hr
- Small biz custom: $10K-$50K | Mid-market: $40K-$150K | Enterprise: $80K-$250K+
- Turnaround: 4-8wk (small), 8-16wk (mid), 16-28wk (enterprise)
- Content = 10-25% of total budget
- Quartermasters anchor: $25K min, $50K-$200K target range

## Feature Injections (Portals/Dashboards)
- Custom portal: $20K-$50K initial (industry avg), $30K-$200K+ for complex
- Annual maintenance: $10K-$25K/yr
- Dev timeline: 3-6 months
- Off-the-shelf alternatives: $129-$299/mo (SPP, SuiteDash)
- Quartermasters anchor: $30K min portal, $50K+ dashboard

## Express Build (Landing Pages / Small Sites)
- Agency landing page: $2,500-$5,000 standard, $5K-$15K premium
- 5-page site: $5K-$10K budget, $10K-$40K premium
- 24-hr delivery = rare differentiator, commands 25-50% rush premium
- $1,500 for 24-hr page = too low for CA boutique, signals freelancer
- Quartermasters anchor: $3,500-$5,000 (24hr), $2,500-$4,000 (standard)

## AI Consulting (RAG + Fine-Tuning)
- AI consultant US rates: $150-$500+/hr, $600-$3,000/day
- RAG end-to-end: $25K-$150K+ (data cleaning = 30-50% of cost)
- Fine-tuning project: $10K-$100K depending on model/scope
- Full AI solution: $50K-$500K+
- Monthly AI retainer: $2K-$50K/mo (tiered by hours)
- SOC2/GDPR adds 20-30% to any AI project
- Quartermasters anchor: $40K-$80K (RAG), $25K-$75K (fine-tune), $100K-$300K (full solution)

## Monthly Maintenance & Recurring Revenue
- Industry avg retainer: $3,200/mo (SE Ranking 2025 survey, n=260)
- 64% of agencies charge <$1K/mo, only 13% charge $2K-$5K/mo
- Retainer clients: 56-month avg tenure vs 24-month project-only
- Maintenance = 15-25% of initial dev cost annually
- Hosting actual cost: Vercel Pro $20/seat = 85-95% gross margin on maintenance
- Quartermasters anchor: $1K-$4K/mo (hosting), $5K-$15K/mo (retainer+AI)

## Key Pricing Principles
- California HQ + Next.js 16 + Three.js + AI = premium tier, never price below mid-market
- Every project -> maintenance upsell path (56-month LTV vs 24-month)
- 24-hour delivery is Blue Ocean -- charge premium, don't discount
- Market shifting to value-based pricing (10-40% of measurable outcomes) for AI
- Eight-figure agencies retain 92% of clients annually vs 78% for seven-figure
